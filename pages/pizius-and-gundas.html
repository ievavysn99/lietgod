Pizius and Gundas

Gods of marriage, sex and intimacy

In the realm of Lithuanian mythology, Pizius and Gundas stand as revered gods of marriage, sex, and intimacy. Their divine influence encompasses the sacred bonds of matrimony and the profound connection shared between partners. Pizius is sought after by men seeking blessings for harmonious unions, while Gundas is revered by women seeking guidance and fulfillment in their marriages.

Pizius, the god of marriage, embodies the qualities of love, commitment, and unity. Men turn to him, seeking his benevolent presence and blessings as they embark on the journey of marriage. Pizius bestows upon them the wisdom and strength to honor and cherish their partners, fostering an enduring bond built on trust, respect, and shared values.

Gundas holds the sacred responsibility of nurturing the feminine aspect of marital relationships. Women seek his guidance and blessings, seeking fulfillment and happiness within their marriages. Gundas empowers women to embrace their roles as loving partners and to cultivate deep emotional connections with their husbands.

In the realm of sexuality and intimacy, Pizius and Gundas guide individuals towards a deep understanding of the sacredness and beauty of physical connection. They inspire couples to explore and embrace the joys of sexual intimacy, nurturing a sense of passion, trust, and mutual pleasure within their unions.

May the blessings of Pizius and Gundas be with you as you navigate the intricacies of marriage, sex, and intimacy. May their wisdom guide you in fostering deep connections, trust, and fulfillment within your partnerships. And may you embrace the divine gifts of love, passion, and unity that they bestow upon those who seek their guidance.
